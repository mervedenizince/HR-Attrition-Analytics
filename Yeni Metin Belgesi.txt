# HR Attrition Analytics - Power BI Project

##  Project Objective
The goal of this project is to analyze employee attrition and identify the key factors that affect workforce stability.  
By visualizing the data with Power BI, HR managers can make data-driven decisions to improve retention strategies.

##  Tools & Skills
- **Power BI** (DAX, Power Query, Data Modeling, Visualization)
- **SQL** (Data extraction and preprocessing)
- **Excel** (Data cleaning and preparation)
